CODEBEAT

Introduction

Codebeat is an audio programming language designed with a flexible syntax and a data-driven model. Take a look at the samples below or write you own. More instructions are available on the project's GitHub page.

Samples

These sample pieces demonstrate the primary features of the language, including chords, slides, and several @effects. Don't forget to try it for yourself.
